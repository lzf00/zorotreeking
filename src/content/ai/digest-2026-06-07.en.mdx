# The Gradient

## <a href="https://thegradient.pub/virtue-ethics-ai-alignment/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">After Orthogonality: Virtue-Ethical Agency and AI Alignment</a>

*The Gradient*

This AI alignment research carried out from the perspective of virtue ethics challenges the default goal-oriented assumption, pointing out that human rationality is not anchored to a fixed final goal, but actions adapt to a self-promoting practice network including action tendencies, evaluation rules and other elements. It proposes that if AI is to collaborate with humans, its decision-making logic needs to be isomorphic with human practical action logic, which can cover both ethical and core safety alignment requirements at the same time.
