## <a href="https://finance.yahoo.com/personal-finance/investing/article/what-is-options-trading-130100763.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">What is options trading?</a>

*2026/7/22 21:01:00*

Options trading is a category of financial derivative trading, the trading target is the right to buy or sell the specified underlying asset at an agreed price at a specific time in the future. The underlying assets can cover stocks, funds, bonds, commodities and other categories. After paying the premium, the option buyer can choose to exercise the right or abandon the exercise, while the seller receives the premium and needs to bear the performance obligation, which can be used for risk hedging, arbitrage and leveraged investment.
